About us

what is this hospital?

This is merely a online hospital that connects rmp doctors with doctors in the cities via video call and chat bot and brings medicines to your home!:-)

Most of the rural areas do not have proper access to medical facilities and proper doctors. So, with the help of technology we aim to provide solutions to this problem and give access to the medical facilities and doctors via online.

We have many doctors in our smart healthcare system and also many rmp doctors to help you.We will keep a track of your health record.

you can directly buy medicines online in our cart.
