How we work

Rmp view

Rmp logs in First

he will be taken to rmp homepage where he can find his profile, meet a doctor option, booking history, patient list , buy

  • First he wil ask patient symtoms.
  • Later he will note details in patient records.
  • Now he will check avaliability of the doctor.
  • He will contact doctor via video call or chatbot as preffered by patient paying the amount.
  • After that he will dowload prescription and then buy medicines in the cart.
  • He will add all details to patient record.

Doctor view

Doctor logins first

Doctor will be directed to doctor homepage after logging in, there he can find his profile,attending patients , work history.

  • He will see patient requests that he received from rmp doctors.
  • He will attend patients via video call or chatbot as requested by the patients.
  • Next he will send a prescription to rmp in a pdf format that is autogenerated after ending call.
  • He can see all his payments details in his invoice page.
  • He can attend other patient after completing this call or chat with this patient.

Medicine Cart

Cart for medicines

Here in this cart you can directly buy medicines which will be delivered at your door.

  • the best part of this cart is when you complete a video call or chatbot with a doctor then a automatically generated prescription pdf with details of tablets will be sent.
  • Medicines will be auto searched in the cart.
  • you can make changes as you wish like you can add any other medicines if needed and you can remove other medicines if needed.
  • you can choose payment mode.
  • Medicines will be delivered at your doorstep.
